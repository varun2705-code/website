Jaguar F-Pace
Engine Options: 2.0L turbocharged 4-cylinder, 3.0L supercharged V6, and a 5.0L supercharged V8 in higher trims.
Performance: 0-60 mph in as little as 4.1 seconds (depending on model)
Interior: Premium materials, spacious cabin, and advanced tech, including the TouchPro Duo infotainment system.
Safety Features: Standard driver-assistance features like adaptive cruise control, lane-keeping assist, and emergency braking.
Fuel Economy: Around 22 MPG city / 27 MPG highway (depending on model and drivetrain).
